Vanilla Cake

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 4 hours (includes cooling)

Yield: 12-14 servings

  • 433g of Cake Flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 340g of unsalted butter
  • 400g of granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs + 2 egg whites
  • 1 tablespoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 360ml of buttermilk

Vanilla Buttercream

340g unsalted butter, 650g confectioners' sugar,
80ml whole milk/heavy cream, 1½ tsp vanilla extract, 1/8 tsp salt

For more prominent vanilla flavor, use homemade vanilla extract.

This vanilla cake batter is moderately thick and fits perfectly in 3 8-inch cake pans.

Leveling the cake layers promises a straight and sturdy layer cake.
